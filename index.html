Welcome to Sharda Dinings

Located opposite Kust Seva Ashram, NH 29, Sharda Dinings is a landmark in Rajendra Nagar, Gorakhpur. We are an established establishment known for serving authentic flavors and providing top-tier hospitality.


Whether you are looking for a Candle-light Dinner, a venue for a Marriage Party, or a Corporate Meeting space with stay options, we have it all under one roof.
